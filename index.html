Blind run - A game for people with visual impairments

We wanted to give people with visual impairments the unique experience of taking a walk guided by a guide dog in the city.
The player just has to press space when the dog starts to bark louder to avoid danger. The game contains a lot of familiar sounds from our city La Paz.
